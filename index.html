Privacy by Design

Your health data is extremely sensitive. That's why KitPass is built with privacy as the foundation, not an afterthought.

On-Device AI Processing

All AI analysis happens locally on your device using Apple's Foundation Models. Your data never leaves your iPhone.

End-to-End Encryption

Your health records are encrypted with industry best standard security by Apple HealthKit. Only you can access your data.

No Data Collection

We don't collect, store, or share your personal health information. Your privacy is guaranteed.
